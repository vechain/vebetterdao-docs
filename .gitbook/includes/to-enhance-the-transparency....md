title: To enhance the transparency...
---

**To enhance the transparency** of the VeBetterDAO platform and x2earn apps, following also the ["dApp Tracker" proposal](https://governance.vebetterdao.org/proposals/48098052949649968323435845011935575242371156821795293696975298342916966237360), we have developed a centralized reward distributor contract. This contract must be used by the apps to ensure that every transfer of a B3TR token related to a sustainable action is publicly tracked and accessible to the community.
