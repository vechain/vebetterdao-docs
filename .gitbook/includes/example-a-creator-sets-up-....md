{% hint style="info" %}
**Example:** A creator sets up a public challenge with a 10,000 B3TR prize pool, 5 winners, and a minimum of 10 actions. At the end, B3MO identifies the 5 participants who completed at least 10 actions first. Those who complete the actions and claim the prize first each receive 2,000 B3TR.&#x20;
{% endhint %}
